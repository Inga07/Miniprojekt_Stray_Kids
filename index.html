Profesional Profile

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2018 through a survival show of the same name. The group is known for its powerful performances, self-produced music, and bold, high-energy sound that blends hip-hop, EDM, and pop.

They have gained international recognition with albums like NOEASY and ★★★★★ (5-STAR), becoming one of the leading fourth-generation K-pop groups worldwide.

Types of Content

self-produced, variety-style & intimate content

SKZ CODE

A popular variety show series where members play games, go on trips, and do themed challenges, often described as their version of "Run BTS"

2 Kids Room / 2 Kids Show

Content focused on pairing two members together to talk about their relationships, personal stories, and dynamics, providing a deeper look at their camaraderie.

Finding Stray Kids

Reality-show content where they compete in various fun, high-energy challenges.

SKZ-PLAYER

Specialized video projects where members release self-produced songs, dance covers, or collaborations that are not formally promoted on music shows.

SKZ-RECORD

Similar to SKZ-PLAYER, but generally focused on audio releases such as original unreleased songs or song covers.

SKZFLIX

A musical short film created by the group that showcases their acting skills.

SKZ-VLOG

Individual vlogs allowing members to share their hobbies and personal lives (e.g., Felix's baking, Lee Know's pet care)

Magazine Pictorials/Endorsements

Individual and group activities in fashion, modeling for high-end brands (e.g., Versace, Louis Vuitton), and commercial endorsement videos.

Dance Practice Videos

Special practice videos, including "black swan" or Halloween costume versions, highlighting their choreography.
